Our Story

e-Qurbanify was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to make Halal Qurbani accessible, trustworthy, and convenient for Muslims across Pakistan and beyond. We recognized the challenges families face during Eid-ul-Adha - concerns about animal quality, Shariah compliance, fair pricing, and hygienic processing. Urban families, in particular, struggled to find reliable sources for Qurbani animals that met both Islamic requirements and modern hygiene standards.

Founded by a team of practicing Muslims with backgrounds in livestock management, Islamic studies, and technology, we set out to bridge the gap between traditional Qurbani practices and the needs of contemporary Muslim families. Our platform combines centuries-old Islamic principles with modern transparency, verification systems, and customer service standards.

Our Mission

To provide a trusted, transparent, and technologically-enabled platform where Muslims can confidently fulfill their Qurbani obligations with complete peace of mind. We aim to uphold the highest standards of Shariah compliance, animal welfare, and customer service, making the blessed act of Qurbani accessible to everyone.

Our Commitment to Islamic Values

Islam is at the heart of everything we do. Every animal on our platform is verified for Qurbani eligibility according to Islamic jurisprudence. Our slaughter process strictly follows the Sunnah method, performed by trained Muslim slaughtermen who understand the spiritual significance of this act. We consult with Islamic scholars regularly to ensure our operations remain compliant with Shariah principles across all major schools of thought (Hanafi, Shafi'i, Maliki, and Hanbali).

Beyond mere compliance, we embrace the deeper spiritual dimensions of Qurbani - the act of sacrifice, submission to Allah, and charity to those in need. This is why we facilitate meat distribution to deserving families, helping you fulfill both the personal and communal aspects of this beautiful act of worship.

Transparency & Trust

In an industry often plagued by mistrust and exploitation, we've made transparency our cornerstone. Every animal listing includes real photographs (never stock images), actual weight measurements, complete health records, and farm locations. Our live video viewing option allows you to see the animal in real-time before purchase - a level of transparency unprecedented in Pakistan's livestock market.

We believe honesty is not just good business; it's an Islamic obligation. As the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) said, "The truthful and trustworthy merchant will be with the prophets, the truthful, and the martyrs." (Tirmidhi). This hadith guides our every transaction.

Animal Welfare

Islam teaches us to be kind to animals. The Prophet (peace be upon him) said, "Whoever is merciful, even to a sparrow, Allah will be merciful to him on the Day of Judgment." (Bukhari). We ensure all animals in our care are treated humanely, kept in clean environments, properly fed, and never subjected to cruelty. Our transportation methods prioritize animal comfort, and our slaughter process is designed to minimize distress.

Modern Technology Meets Sunnah

While we deeply respect traditional practices, we embrace technology as a tool to serve the Ummah better. Our platform makes it possible for overseas Pakistanis to book Qurbani for their families back home. Busy professionals can research and select animals at their convenience. Real-time tracking keeps you informed throughout the process. Technology, when used correctly, amplifies our ability to serve Allah and His creation.
